Popular LIC Plans

LIC Jeevan Anand

A perfect blend of protection and savings, Jeevan Anand offers life cover throughout life along with a lump sum payout at the end of the policy term — ensuring both security and financial growth.

LIC Tech Term Plan

An online pure protection plan that offers high life cover at an affordable premium. Ideal for digitally savvy individuals seeking comprehensive protection for their family.

LIC New Endowment Plan

This plan combines savings and protection with guaranteed returns. It helps you save systematically while ensuring financial support for your loved ones in your absence.

LIC Money Back Plan

A popular plan that provides periodic returns during the policy term, making it ideal for those who want liquidity along with insurance coverage.

LIC Jeevan Lakshya

Designed especially for parents, this plan ensures your child’s financial stability even in your absence. It provides a combination of income benefits and maturity sum assured.

LIC Jeevan Umang

A lifetime income plan that offers annual survival benefits after the premium payment term, along with life cover — making it perfect for long-term financial security.
